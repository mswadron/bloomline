Terms of Use & Community Guidelines

Last updated: July 8, 2026
The short version: Be kind and accurate, post only your own content, don't reveal exact locations of rare plants, and don't rely on Bloomline to decide what's safe to eat or touch. We can remove content and accounts that break these rules.

1. Using Bloomline

Bloomline is a free community field guide. By using it you agree to these terms. You must be old enough to consent in your country (at least 13, or 16 where required). You're responsible for activity under your account.

2. Community guidelines

When you post field notes, links, or sightings, please:

  • Keep it relevant to plants, nature, and the route context.
  • Be respectful — no harassment, hate speech, threats, or spam.
  • Post only content you created or have the right to share, and links that are safe and on-topic.
  • Don't post personal information about yourself or others.
  • Don't post false, misleading, or commercial/promotional content.
Protect sensitive species. Do not pin the precise location of rare, endangered, threatened, or protected plants. Leave the location off, or keep it general. Publishing exact locations can expose these plants to poaching, trampling, and collection.

Misidentification can cause serious harm or death. Always confirm with a qualified expert.
